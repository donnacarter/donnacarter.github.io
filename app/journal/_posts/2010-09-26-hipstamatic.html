I have been having a lot of fun with a new app for my Iphone recently, it is called Hipstamatic. It makes taking photographs with your phone a whole lot more interesting!

It gives your digital photos a rustic analog feel. The app enables you to change the lens and flash to a variety which is included within the app, you can also buy more if you wish. Here are some of the photos I have taken so far.
