I recently went on holiday to Rome, I had the best time ever. Before I got there though I had a bit of a drama – as you normally do when packing the night before you are due to catch your flight!

I grabbed hold of my camera and then went on a frantic hunt around the flat for the charger, which I still have not found by the way… I had to just give up in the end and I was devastated at the thought of not being able to take any photographs of my trip to Rome, I would literally feel naked without it. Then I saw my Diana F+ sat there, it is a good job I had plenty of film!

I easily used 4 rolls of film, one of which was the LomoChrome Purple. Since purple is my favourite colour I was super excited about this one and saved it for when I was going to be at the Colosseum. I thought shooting ruins in purple would be perfect.

Anyway here are some of the photos from the LomoChrome Purple film – super pleased with them!
