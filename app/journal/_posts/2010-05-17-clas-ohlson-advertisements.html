Recently I can't help but notice that Clas Ohlson have really gone to town on their advertising campaign.

I mean it is brilliant to see so much effort and thought going into the launch of their new shop in Leeds, not many places seem to do this. What I like best about the ads is the fact that they are really bold and hard to miss. It is a really brave attempt of using humour to advertise their products which is potentially risky to do.
