Recently I went on a mini holiday to Krakow, it is such an amazing place to visit.

What really caught my eye was the signage there. There was such a mix of modern – the kind of signs you might see on the trendy places to eat in London – and really old fashioned more traditional looking signs.

Anyway I took a bunch of photos of some!
