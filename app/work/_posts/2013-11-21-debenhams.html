To showcase their new coffee offering Debenhams wanted a series of friendly and engaging posters. The four posters are then to be used in all of their in-store cafes.

Debenhams wanted to go for a colourful and illustrative approach, something new and fresher feeling which really talked about the coffee. With this in mind I started playing around with ideas which focussed on how much love, care and attention goes in to the growing & making the coffee.

I created simple, light hearted illustrations to visually represent the concepts in the text. A hand-written style font and a natural but bright palette were used to capture an organic & friendly feel, which represents the ethical sourcing of the beans as well as the care that is taken when brewing the coffee.
