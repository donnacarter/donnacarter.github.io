The London Coffee guide is the definitive guide to London's coffee shops, thousands of copies are sold every year to coffee enthusiasts. So I was extremely excited to be designing an advert to go inside this fantastic, widely distributed book!

The United Coffee advert was printed in the 2012 edition and was to promote their place in the coffee industry. It was important to maintain brand consistency but also to design something that was eye-catching and playful.

The Make Decent Coffee advert was printed in the 2013 edition. It was intended to introduce consumers to the new website, whilst also making them aware of what Make Decent Coffee is all about. Make Decent Coffee is a bold, playful brand which clearly shines through in the advert.
