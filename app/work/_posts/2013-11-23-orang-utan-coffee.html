The Sumatran orangutan is under threat of extinction through mass deforestation, the Orang Utan Coffee Project encourages smallholders and their farming communities to cultivate their plantations sustainably and to commit to the preservation of the Rainforest.

Waitrose have got on board with this project and wanted the coffee available throughout their stores. Using the Orang Utan Coffee brand guidelines I designed a premium and simple bag which would sit nicely on the shelves at Waitrose.
