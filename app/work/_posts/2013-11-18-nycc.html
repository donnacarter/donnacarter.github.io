Inspired by his experiences in New York City and his love of coffee, Peter Andre had decided to open a coffee shop called New York Coffee Club. Our role was to build a brand around his concept.

Peter was looking for a logo that both captured his influence of New York City coffee shops and could be a recognisable brand amongst the high-street coffee shops. I began by exploring various logo ideas that embraced aspects of connections to New York and Americana.

{% include breakout-image-right-caption.html image_src='work/nycc/nycc-ideas.jpg' image_alt='The chosen logo' caption='Initial logo concepts.' %}

We submitted 6 brand approaches to his team and one of mine was chosen to be developed as the final brand. The onechosen was a simple one that balanced retro and contemporary - with the lone star implying the American influence.

{% include breakout-image-right-caption.html image_src='work/nycc/nycc-logo1.png' image_alt='The chosen logo' caption='The final logo and colour palette.' %}

I applied the brand to a wide variety of media including environment concepts, exterior signage, cups, sugar sticks, uniform and business cards.

{% include breakout-image-right-caption.html image_src='work/nycc/nycc-shop-front.jpg' image_alt='Front of the coffee shop' caption='I worked closely with the shopfitter to create the signage for the shop front.' %} {% include breakout-image-right-caption.html image_src='work/nycc/nycc-cards.jpg' image_alt='NYCC business card and loyalty card' caption='Loyalty card and business card designs.' %} {% include breakout-image-right-caption-pair.html image_1_src='work/nycc/nycc-cups.jpg' image_1_alt='A couple of branded takeout cups' image_2_src='work/nycc/nycc-cup-macro.jpg' image_2_alt='The tagline on the side of the cup' caption='Takeout cups came in 3 different sizes, each size used a different colour from the colour palette along with a different tag line on the back.' %} {% include breakout-image-right-caption.html image_src='work/nycc/nycc-cupcakes.jpg' image_alt='NYCC logo on cupcakes' %}

The brand later branched into retail and I was asked to design packaging for a coffee to be stocked in Tescos nationwide. NYCC would be working in partnership with The Peter Andre Foundation and Cancer Research UK, a donation would go to these charities with every bag sold.

{% include breakout-image-below-caption.html image_src='work/nycc/nycc-bags.jpg' image_alt='NYCC bags of coffee' %}

Due to work with two seperate charities, this project had quite a strict brief and required a lot of consideration of copy. It was important to work closely with both charities to make sure they were happy with the design and use of their logos.
