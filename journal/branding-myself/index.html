Branding myself

20.05.10

A while ago now I decided that I would try and brand myself, by that I don't mean getting out a hot piece of metal and actually branding/burning myself! No I wanted to try and create an identity for myself, something I could use on websites and various other things.

I want to show you some of the ideas I had before I decided to stick with the one I have, getting the pens and pencils out to draw out my ideas is something that I enjoy immensly and hardly ever do for some reason. This will change! I now carry my pens and pencils everywhere with me along with a notebook to draw when ever I like (plus I seem to have design ideas while I'm out and about then forget by the time I get home haha).
