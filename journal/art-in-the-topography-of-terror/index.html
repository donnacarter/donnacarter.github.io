Art in the Topography of Terror

22.10.10

So here I am on holiday in Berlin, I have been to learn about the history of Berlin which is truly astonishing and interesting.

What I didn't think I would find amongst all the history in the Topography of Terror was some very old but interesting advertisements, posters and newspapers. I was amazed that such things existed still. The typography and illustrations were brilliant. I managed to get some examples for you all to see.
