The Letterpress Monster

08.06.12

I just wanted to share this really cool website with everyone!

The Letterpress Monster is in fact Ian Gabb, he is a designer/printer and is currently working as a letterpress technician at the Royal College of Art in London. His new site is a great source of inspiration, it is not only a showcase of his work but also acts as a scrapbook of various printed items he has collected!

Go and check it out; www.letterpressmonster.com
