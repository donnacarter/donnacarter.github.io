The Typography Manual

24.05.10

The itunes app store is a complete minefield when it comes to finding anything specific, I find myself endlessly searching until I eventually get lost in the app world and give up. Not so long ago I found this amazing app called "The Typography Manual".

It has since become a little pocket manual for anything typography related, a history of type, a guide for using type on the web and a bunch of other things too that you will have to see for yourself. I recommend this to any designers out there, it is one of the best design apps I have found so far.
