It's not how good you are...

22.08.10

It's not how good you are, it's how good you want to be. I am always amazed when I am talking to my designer friends, of how few of them have heard of this book! This book should be every designers bible, the book itself may not look very big or interesting but it is possibly the most inspiring/motivational book I have read. It is a must read for any designer. It gives you no nonsense advice delivered in a casual/humorous way. Extremely easy to read.

Two of my favourite quotes from the book are;

Fail, fail again, fail better

Samuel Beckett

There are no short cuts to any place worth going.

Beverly Sills
