London Types: Graphic Design At CSM’s New Granary Building

11.09.13

Walking around Granary Square near Kings cross the other weekend I happened to stumble across a really cool little exhibition that was on. The exhibition is free and runs along the windows of Central Saint Martins, pretty cool huh?!

This exhibition, organised by Show Us Your Type, is an international art project showcasing and relating type and cities. Designers submit pieces based on a typographical representation of London. A very cool idea and there are some fantastic pieces of work there, well worth a look.

It is only a small exhibition but worth seeing, here are some more photos I took.
