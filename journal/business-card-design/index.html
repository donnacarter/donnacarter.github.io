Business card design

06.08.10

So recently I have have been working on designs for some personal business cards. I have finally sent them to print I am really excited and can't wait to see the end result!

I have opted to go for a more expensive route and get a Spot Gloss applied to one side of the business card, the spot gloss will spell out my name on top of my favourite colour of all time to give a subtle but elegant and professional feel.

The printing company I decided to use are www.thelocalprintcompany.co.uk they have been extremely friendly and helpful and would definitely recommend them to others seeking a printers.
