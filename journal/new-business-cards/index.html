New business cards

25.09.14

I never really have much use for business cards; I just really like to have them. You never know when they might come in handy I guess! I also thought it would be a fun little side project for me to distract myself with.

My aim was to keep them simple and attractive (obviously), while trying out something that allowed me to get a bit creative. So I went and had my logo made into a stamp, bought a couple of ink pads… But what next? WELL Moo.com have a range of “Luxe Business Cards” which are really thick card – three times thicker to be exact - with a rich seam of colour which runs through the middle of the card. Perfect.

Here are the results, I am super pleased with them and thoroughly enjoyed getting to play with the stamp!
