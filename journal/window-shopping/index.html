Window shopping

14.05.10

Not a lot of shop windows really stand out and grab your attention, which is very disappointing as there is so much potential to sell through window dressing. As I was walking through town I saw an amazing shop window, it instantly made me stop in my tracks to stand and read what it had to say.

The shop window in question was Lush, the vibrant colours and and striking “in your face” type is really hard to ignore. I just had to share this with everyone, I have also taken a photograph of the other window which was equally as interesting.
