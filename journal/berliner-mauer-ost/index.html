Berliner Mauer (OST)

31.01.11

I found this free of charge brush font the other day and thought it was really quite awesome.

The font was inspired from the graffiti on the Berlin Wall (the eastern side), created by Bratislav Milenkovic, see more of his work here. Having been to Berlin and walked along the Berlin wall myself I found this truly inspirational. I can't wait for this to be available for download!

Please check out the post for yourself at Typography Served.
