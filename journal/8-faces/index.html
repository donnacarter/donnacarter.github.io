8 Faces

09.08.10

I have been so engrossed with various things recently that I totally forgot to share this with you all!

A friend linked me to this site/magazine which is very exciting (well if you like type). Sadly I have missed out on the actual magazine, it sold out in two hours, but I think I will be buying the PDF version.

The site is http://8faces.com/

The magazine features 8 leading designers who get asked the question, “If you could only use eight typefaces for the rest of your life, which would you choose?”. The magazine is made out to be more of a collectors item rather then a magazine.
