My Diana F+ - round 2

28.08.12

I finally got around to getting 2 more films developed! As these films were now almost a year old I actually had no idea what was going to be on them, double the surprise woo hoo... I decided to get each film processed in a different way so that I could experiment and see what effect the different processes would have.

I am so pleased at the results, the photos have come out really well and it looks like I have finally got the hang of using the camera too. I decided to post some of my favourites here but if you want to see the whole set they are on my flickr: www.flickr.com
