Coopers

Branding, Website

When UCC Coffee brought Coopers under their wing, they decided it would be a great idea to give them a face lift. As a former employee of Coopers I was really excited to be leading the development of the rebrand.

The old Coopers branding felt outdated, it didn't really represent how Coopers wanted to appear as a company. The challenge was to create a new identity for Coopers that would help them be a fun, friendly and approachable company, giving them a fresh lease of life.

Once the brand guidelines had been completed it was time to apply this to various mediums such as the new office environment, stationary, packaging, canvas’ and various promotional materials.

After the big re-brand Coopers decided it was time to redevelop their website. My role in this project was to design, develop and produce content for the site while colloborating with a developer for the back-end programming tasks.

The site is designed to be minimal, spacious and suited to a variety of screen sizes. Since launch the site has been featured on a number of web design galleries.
