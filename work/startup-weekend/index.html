Startup Weekend

Promotional Materials

Startup Weekend is a weekend-long event where aspiring entrepreneurs come together to discuss their ideas and have the opportunity to meet some really inspirational people.

I was asked by the event organisers of Startup Weekend London to give their sponsorship document a revamp for the upcoming Mobile Edition event.

This document was needed to encourage large technology companies to sponsor the event, so really should look fantastic and show that Startup Weekend is worth sponsoring. There was a lot of important information to make visually attractive and easy to take in.

Once the sponsorship document was completed I also designed brand assets for the event such as posters, t-shirts and web banners.
