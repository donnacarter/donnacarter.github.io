Me

Subtitle text

I am a bit of an all rounder designer really, my current role involves me working with print, web, packaging projects and more - I love how this challenges me. So enough about work, what else do I enjoy...

Working in the coffee industry has turned me into a bit of a coffee fan/snob/geek/connoisseur, so one thing that I have learnt is how to make a great cup of coffee!

While I am sat drinking my coffee I am probably finding my next font to buy. I have a serious love of type and probably have far too many fonts on my mac. Here are a few of the best places I get fonts from.

  • www.hypefortype.com
  • www.youworkforthem.com
  • www.fontsquirrel.com
  • www.myfonts.com

One of my favourite possessions is my beloved Diana F+ camera which I keep loaded up with film – yes film – ready to take with me when I go somewhere interesting. You really can’t beat the excitement of getting film developed to see how your photos came out, I am slowly getting better at this, it is surprisingly hard.

Travelling to work in trendy Shoreditch everyday has really opened my eyes to all the art which is around in the streets. I started shoreditchgraffiti.co.uk to keep a diary of sorts of all the great graffiti that comes and goes.

Also when I am having a rough day or I am finding something tricky, this quote really helps. It is also a really great book that everyone should read.

It's not how good you are, it's how good you want to be.
