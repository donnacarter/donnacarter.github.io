Me

I am a bit of an all rounder designer really, my current role involves me working with print, web, packaging projects and more - I love how this challenges me. So enough about work, what else do I enjoy...

Working with coffee has turned me into a bit of a fan/snob/geek/connoisseur, so one thing that I have learnt is how to make a great cup of coffee!

One of my favourite possessions is my beloved Diana F+ camera which I keep loaded up with film – yes film – ready to take with me when I go somewhere interesting.

I started shoreditchgraffiti.co.uk to keep a diary of sorts of all the great graffiti that comes and goes in Shoreditch.

Also when I am having a rough day, reminding myself of this really helps.
