I was born in York in the summer of 1986 and grew up there under the aroma of the nearby Nestl√© chocolate factory. At school I aspired to be a scientist. That is, until I chose to study Graphic Design over Design Technology. That's when I found my occupation. Science would have to be a backup plan.

I studied for a degree in Graphic Design at the University of Huddersfield. There I really broadened my knowledge and skills. I found myself inspired by the works of Bauhaus and blah blah. Another sentence will fill this space up a bit more than this bit of filler I have just typed

After university I joined the Coffee Industry, working as an in-house designer. I soon found myself transitioning from a casual drinker to a coffee snob. These days my favourite brew is a nice single-origin brewed with a V60 pourover.

In 2011 I moved from Yorkshire to London to join a design studio. There, I broadened my skill-set with larger projects and working with more digital design. Having moved around the city a couple of times, I have now settled in Rotherhithe where I enjoy the contrast of canals and wildlife against the backdrop of skyscrapers.
